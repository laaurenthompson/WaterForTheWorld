About us

Water for the World is a non-profit organization which provides clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries in Africa.

Our journey began in 2005 and since that time we have managed to help millions of needy and helpless people living in poverty and poor conditions.

We believe that everyone has a right to standard living conditions regardless of their situation.
