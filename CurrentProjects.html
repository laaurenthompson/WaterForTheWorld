We are currently working on a variety of projects across Africa, implementing wells in communities who need it most

Ghana

We are currently working on installing a number of Wells in Ghana

Some of our wells are located between villages, ensuring those in the areas surrounding the Well don't have to travel so far for clean water

Nigeria

We have been running local groups to educate members of the public about clean water

Given the highly religious demographic in Nigera, we are holding church groups to ensure widespread availability of information

Sudan

Distribution of Purification Tablets

In partnership with the local authorities and government, we have distributed Purification Tablets amongst the population to ensure access to clean, healthy water

Gambia

Continuous investment in infrastructure

Working closely with our supporters and fundraisers, we have a steady supply stream of aid in to the infrastructure of Gambia, ensuring sanitation plants are operating at full capacity
